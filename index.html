So-Called Philanthropist

2019, Canvas, silk stockings, acrylic, 100x100cm

Multiple materials acrylic painting.

Depicts an ostensive-cetacean, peeling off his mask, who is in fact the mastermind behind the killing of whales.The multi-material of this painting is reflected in the use of silk stockings. The fleshy stockings perfectly presents the texture of human skin, which at the same time give the viewers a sense of vision and touch.
